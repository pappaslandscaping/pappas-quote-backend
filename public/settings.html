Square Payments

Accept card, ACH & Apple Pay payments on invoices
Checking...

Square enables your customers to pay invoices online with credit/debit cards, ACH bank transfers, and Apple Pay. When you send an invoice, customers receive a secure payment link.

Environment: Location:

Setup Required

  1. Create a Square Developer account at developer.squareup.com
  2. Create an application and get your credentials
  3. Add these environment variables to your server:
    SQUARE_ACCESS_TOKEN, SQUARE_APPLICATION_ID, SQUARE_LOCATION_ID
  4. Set SQUARE_ENVIRONMENT to sandbox or production
  5. Restart the server
